Privacy Policy – Voxly

Effective Date: July 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you log in with Facebook and authorize our app, we may collect the following information:

  • Your public Facebook profile (name, profile picture, email)
  • Instagram account details (business account ID, username, etc.)
  • Access tokens required to post content on your behalf

We do not collect your password or any unrelated data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Authenticate you via Facebook Login
  • Enable content publishing to your Instagram Business Account
  • Manage and track your post requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except where required by law or necessary to provide our service.

3. Data Retention

We securely store tokens and associated data only as long as necessary for service delivery. If you revoke permissions or delete your account, we will delete the associated data.

4. Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time via your Facebook settings:

https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools

5. Security

We use HTTPS encryption and secure token storage to protect your data.

6. Contact Us

For any questions or privacy-related concerns, contact us at: support@voxly.app
